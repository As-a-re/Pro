Common Conditions and Symptoms

1. Malaria

Headache, Stomach pains, Nausea, Dizziness, Feverishness

2. Breast Cancer

Breast lump, Rash around nipple, Swelling in armpit, Discharge from nipple, Change in structure of breast

3. Diabetes

Feeling very thirsty, Feeling very tired, Blurred vision, Itching around private parts, Slow healing of wounds

4. Food Poisoning

Nauseous, Vomiting, Diarrhoea, Chills, Weakness, Abdominal pain, High temperature

5. Prostate Cancer

Pain in testicles, Frequent urinating, Difficulty in urinating, Feeling of bladder not emptied after urinating

6. Stomach Ulcer

Stomach pains, Weight loss, Indigestion, Feeling sick, Loss of appetite
